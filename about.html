Our History

Our Journey of Excellence in Dedicated Home Care

1

A Foundation of 10+ Years Experience

Verola Care was founded by Esther Wachira, drawing on over a decade of hands-on expertise across health and social care, supported living, and domiciliary care. Built from a deep, genuine passion for helping others, the company was created with a clear objective: to improve lives by making quality, individualized care accessible to the community.

2

Where Care Meets Personal Connection

We recognized early on that care is deeply personal. It is never just about completing a list of daily tasks—it is about building meaningful companionship, cultivating trust, and making people feel thoroughly safe and valued. We approach the huge responsibility of entering a client's home with exceptional warmth and integrity.

3

Expanding the Horizon of Support

Our journey begins with outstanding domiciliary care services, empowering individuals to stay independent in familiar surroundings. Looking ahead, Verola Care is actively scaling to incorporate comprehensive supported living systems, ensuring that as our clients' needs evolve, our dedication to their encouragement and dignity remains unshakeable.

Take the First Step Toward Reliable Home Care

At Velora Care Group, we provide person-centred supported living services that empower adults to live independently while receiving the right level of support. We work alongside individuals to build confidence, develop life skills, maintain wellbeing, and achieve their personal goals in a safe and supportive environment.

Our domiciliary care services offer the professional support your family needs, ensuring your loved one retains their independence, dignity, and comfort right at home in Preston.
