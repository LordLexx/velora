At Velora Care, we are building a team of compassionate, dedicated, and reliable individuals who share our passion for providing high-quality, person-centred support. We believe our staff are our greatest asset and are committed to creating a positive, supportive, and rewarding working environment.

As we grow, we will be looking for Support Workers, Senior Support Workers, Team Leaders, and Registered Managers who are committed to promoting independence, dignity, and choice. If you are passionate about making a difference in people’s lives, we would love to hear from you.
