Mission-Focused

At Velora Care, our vision is to provide high-quality supported living and community-based support for adults with autism, learning disabilities and complex mental health needs. We are committed to empowering individuals to live independently, make meaningful choices and achieve their personal goals within their communities.

We are currently preparing for CQC registration and look forward to offering supported living and domiciliary care services across the North West.

Why Choose Velora Care Group?

  • Decades of collective healthcare and clinical experience
  • Bespoke care plans tailored entirely around your unique routines
  • Small, dedicated teams to ensure wonderful continuity of care
  • Full compliance with rigorous national UK care standards

Our Core Services

Personal Care Support

Following CQC registration, Velora Care will deliver compassionate personal care that supports dignity, privacy, and independence.

Medication

Following CQC registration, Velora Care will support individuals to manage their medication safely and independently in accordance with their care plans.

Companionship & Visits

Dedicated social interaction, accompanying to local Preston appointments, and warm conversation to prevent isolation.

Meal Prep & Nutrition

Planning and cooking fresh, nutritious meals customized to dietary requirements and personal food preferences.

Domestic Assistance

Light housekeeping duties, including laundry, changing bed linens, and keeping living spaces clean and secure.

Reablement & Recovery

Short-term care pathways to help individuals regain confidence and independent physical skills following a hospital discharge.

Take the First Step Toward Reliable Home Care

At Velora Care Group, we provide person-centred supported living services that empower adults to live independently while receiving the right level of support. We work alongside individuals to build confidence, develop life skills, maintain wellbeing, and achieve their personal goals in a safe and supportive environment.

Our domiciliary care services offer the professional support your family needs, ensuring your loved one retains their independence, dignity, and comfort right at home in Preston.
